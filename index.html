Climate Reality Leadership

On the 26th July 2020, I finished the training of the Climate Reality Leadership Corps and become an official Climate Activist. I emphasise "official" because before this training, I have been focus on working with environmental education. Now I will have more knowledge and capacity to be an advocate for Climate Justice. I learned throughout the course that climate change is a global issue but affects the minorities and marginal communities more intensely.

Moving to Egypt around a year ago, from the moment I stepped in this land, I noticed that care for the environment is an afterthought. It is common to see piles of trash on the street, floating on the sea and being burned as a way to get rid of it. People are not aware of how this affects them and that they have the power to make a difference. As a Global Shaper and a Biologist, with a master’s degree in Ecology, this was very unsettling to me. In an attempt to improve the environment I have organized beach clean ups and talks to demonstrate how climate change affects us and how little steps in our everyday life make a big difference.

With the existing knowledge from my field of study, along with being a climate advocate, I am convinced that I can help to create better strategies, and will connect me with other inspirational young leaders that I can learn from and network with.
