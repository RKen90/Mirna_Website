Academic Experience

2013-2015 Volunteer: Ornithology Lab Volunteer Program – Universidade Federal do Acre – Acre, Brazil.

Studied the association of ticks and birds. This project was done in a collaboration with other ornithologists. The objective was to study if the avian fauna was being committed by ticks and if these ticks had spotted fever, which could be passed to the birds. This study resulted in a paper entitled “Ticks and tick-associated spotted fever group Rickettsia from birds in the Southwestern Brazilian Amazon”.


2013 – 2014: “Biocamp- Integrating knowledge and wisdom”. Universidade Federal do Acre – Acre, Brazil (Hours: 320).

Lecturer and promoter of Scientific initiation to high school students. This project was done in a collaboration with other students and teachers. The objective of the project was to introduce general knowledge about the scientific methods and studies to High School children and also to shape young citizens so that they are aware of the importance of preserving the biodiversity and its natural habitat.


2012-2012: “Animals in School” Universidade Federal do Acre – Acre, Brazil (Hours – 180).

Lecturer for public school children about the ecology of mammals of the State of Acre. The objective of these lecturers were to promote the knowledge about the mammal fauna of the state, since children normally only know mammals that are not Brazilian. Also, the lectures talked about the importance of preservation of the species.
